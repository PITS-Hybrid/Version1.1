Paisa Manager Pro

We are team of youth from various background and skill set work together for a common purpose- to make significant change in life of others. Paisa Manager is also such an idea that came out in one informal discussion session. As we sat and dug deep, we saw potential in this idea - to make significant change in financial habit of individual. To make this reality we- FinLit (financial literacy initiator) and Krennova (an IT company), all lead by youth, worked together as team to make it happen.
